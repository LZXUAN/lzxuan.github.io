ABOUT ME

LIM ZHI XUAN

I am Lim Zhi Xuan, a student currently living in Taiping, a town in Perak, Malaysia which has a special name called "rain town". I am currently studying at SMJK Hua Lian, Taiping.

When I am 12 years old, my computer teacher introduced me to HTML (HyperText Markup Language), a markup language for describing web documents. I immediately became interested and started to learn programming from tutorials on the Internet. I fell in love with programming and web designing ever since.

I love the feeling of satisfaction and joy every time seeing my hard works finally pay off.
